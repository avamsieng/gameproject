Gameplay Instructions

Objective: Make decisions that shape the relationship between Eleanor and Poppy over five school days. Each choice reveals more about who you truly are.

  • Use the mouse to select dialogue options.
  • Save your progress at different points in the story.
  • Different choices lead to different outcomes.
  • Your decisions determine the final revelation.

Tip: Pay attention to Poppy's reactions — they reflect more than you think.
