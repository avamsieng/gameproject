About the Game

In You Are What You Believe, you play as Eleanor — a student who bullies her classmate Poppy across five intense school days. Each choice you make changes the story, shaping Eleanor's personality and her relationship with Poppy. But the deeper you go, the more the truth unfolds — until you come to a realization...
